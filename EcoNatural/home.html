About Us

We believe in being completely eco-friendly from the time the ingredients are harvested to when the soap is used and down the drain. No plastics, no chemicals, no harm.

Quality products are important to us. All scents are tested and engineered to be soothing, and you can expect deliciously aromatic seasonal scents as well!

Pick up your EcoNatural soap in-store at one of our many participating retailers, or contact us for information on our direct-to-door shipping program.
